Our Story and Mission

Pacific Trails Resort was founded in 1984 by Helena and Mark Wilson. The couple first met in the city of Chicago and realized they shared a passion for the outdoors as well as a dream of escaping their urban lifestyles. On a whim, they decided to pack all their things into a camper van and travel the United States. After visiting many different states, the couple had arrived at the California coast and instantly fell in love with the breathtaking scenery. From the grand, mountainous terrain covered in lush forests to the stunning Pacific ocean views, the couple found it difficult to leave. And so they didn't. The Wilsons invite all nature-lovers to put a pause on their stressful city lives and experience the wonders of this idyllic landscape.


"Nature allows us ground ourselves and remember the simple joys of life. At Pacific Trails Resort, we believe that taking a break from the noise of everyday life is essential for reconnecting with ourselves and the world around us. Take a moment to slow down, breathe, and embrace the beauty that nature has to offer. It is our greatest hope that Pacific Trails Resort becomes your place of rejuvenation." - Helena and Mark Wilson

FAQs

When do I check in or check out?

Check-in times have a window of 11am-2pm. You must check out by 10 am on the last day of your stay. Please contact us if any accommodations are needed.

What is the cancellation policy?

To cancel your reservation, please call us during our business hours at (888) 555-5555. A refund will be issued if the cancellation is before 48 hours of your check-in time. If it is after 48 hours before your check-in time, a $25 late fee is applied and there are no refunds.

Are dogs allowed?

Your pup is welcome to join you on this camping experience! Please remember to keep your dog on a leash at all times and pick up after your pet. Complimentary dog treats are offered at the front desk.
